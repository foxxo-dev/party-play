PARTY PLAY.
THE BEST PARTY SONGS,
ARE YOURS

Party Play is an all in one app, where you can either host a party or add your song to one! Whether you want to dance, sing along, or simply have a good time, Party Play has got you covered. Become a host and create your own party playlist or add your favorite songs to an existing party. Let the music unite us all!

How it works

1. Become the host to generate a scannable qr code, or link!
2. Share the qr code, or print it on the wall!
3. Look at your playlist slowly fill up, and the people cheer up!
